Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Bloopy?
Drag to aim your ball, then release to launch. Clear all the orange pegs to win the level! You have a limited number of balls, so aim carefully and use bounces strategically.
What do the different colored pegs do?
  • Orange pegs: Your main targets - clear all of these to win
  • Blue pegs: Regular pegs that give points
  • Green pegs: Special pegs that give you an extra ball
  • Purple pegs: Power-up pegs that activate your selected ability
How do power-ups work?
Before starting a level, select a power-up from the main menu. When your ball hits a purple power-up peg during gameplay, your selected ability activates:
  • Multiball: Splits into multiple balls
  • Fireball: Burns through pegs without bouncing
  • Big Ball: Grows larger to hit more pegs
  • Explosive: Creates chain reactions that clear nearby pegs
How do I unlock new power-ups?
Progress through the campaign levels to unlock additional power-ups. Each power-up is unlocked after completing specific levels.
Can I create my own levels?
Yes! Bloopy includes a built-in level editor. From the main menu, tap the "Editor" button to design your own puzzles. You can place pegs, add obstacles, and test your creations.
Does Bloopy require an internet connection?
No! Bloopy works completely offline. Play anywhere, anytime - no internet required.

Troubleshooting

App won't launch or crashes

  • Make sure your device is running iOS 16.0 or later
  • Try closing the app completely and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Check if there's an app update available in the App Store
  • If problems persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app (note: this will reset your progress)

No sound or music

  • Check that your device isn't in Silent Mode (check the side switch)
  • Go to Settings in the app and make sure Sound and Music are enabled
  • Check your device volume

Game is running slowly

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device to free up memory
  • Make sure you have enough storage space on your device

Progress not saving

  • Make sure you're completing levels (reaching victory or game over)
  • Don't force-close the app while playing
  • Check that your device has available storage
