Privacy Policy

Bloopy - Peg-Popping Puzzle Fun

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Bloopy ("the App") is a mobile game developed for iOS devices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

The Short Version: Bloopy does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your game data stays on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Bloopy is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Payment or financial information

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Level completion status, high scores, and star ratings
  • Settings Preferences: Sound, music, and haptic feedback preferences
  • Custom Levels: Any levels you create in the level editor

This data is stored using iOS's standard UserDefaults system and the app's Documents directory. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

Third-Party Services

Bloopy does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

Bloopy does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted externally, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • iOS encryption
  • App sandboxing

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data
  • Portability: Custom levels can be exported as JSON files

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
